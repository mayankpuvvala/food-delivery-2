Nutrient Information

Protein

Protein is an essential macronutrient that helps build muscle, repair tissue, and make enzymes and hormones. Foods high in protein include:

Lean meats, poultry, fish, eggs, dairy products, beans, lentils, and nuts.

Carbohydrates

Carbohydrates are a macronutrient that provides energy for the body. Foods high in carbohydrates include:

Fruits, vegetables, breads, pasta, and rice.

Fats

Fats are a macronutrient that provides energy and helps the body absorb vitamins. Foods high in healthy fats include:

Avocados, nuts, seeds, and fatty fish.

Vitamins

Vitamins are essential micronutrients that the body needs in small amounts to function properly. Different vitamins have different functions and can be found in a variety of foods.

Minerals

Minerals are essential micronutrients that the body needs in small amounts to function properly. Different minerals have different functions and can be found in a variety of foods.

Fiber

Fiber is a type of carbohydrate that the body can't digest. It helps regulate the body's use of sugars and helps keep hunger and blood sugar in check. Foods high in fiber include:

Fruits, vegetables , whole grains, and legumes.

Water

Water is essential for life and is necessary for many bodily functions. It is important to drink enough water to stay hydrated. Water can be found in beverages and foods such as fruits and vegetables.
